What We Do

We meet families where they are — with programs designed to uplift, educate, and connect.

👶

Early Childhood Support

Resources and programming for parents and caregivers of young children, including developmental screenings, play groups, and parent education workshops.

💚

Family Wellness

Access to mental health resources, wellness workshops, and family counseling connections. Because healthy families start with healthy support systems.

🤝

Community Building

Creating spaces where families connect, share experiences, and build lasting support networks through community events, meetups, and mentorship pairings.

📚

Education Workshops

Free and low-cost workshops covering financial literacy, parenting skills, nutrition, career development, and topics that families actually need.

🏠

Resource Navigation

Helping families find and access existing community resources — from housing assistance to childcare subsidies to food programs — without the runaround.

🌟

Youth Enrichment

After-school programs, mentorship opportunities, and creative outlets for kids and teens to explore their potential in a safe, supportive environment.

Our Story

Founded by Jasmine, Built for Everyone

The WellMade Foundation was born from a simple but powerful belief: every family deserves a strong foundation. Our founder, Jasmine, saw firsthand how community support — or the lack of it — shapes the trajectory of families and children.

After years of working with families in her community and witnessing gaps in access to basic resources, Jasmine created WellMade to be the bridge. Not a handout, but a hand up. A place where families find the tools, connections, and encouragement they need to build the lives they envision.

"WellMade" isn't just our name — it's our standard. We believe that well-made families build well-made communities. And that starts with showing up for each other.

We take your trust seriously. Here's how we ensure every dollar is well spent.

    Financial Reporting — We publish annual reports detailing how funds are raised and allocated across our programs.
